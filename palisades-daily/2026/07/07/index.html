Fire/Rebuild Watch
  • First factory-built home installed in Pacific Palisades. The Santa Monica Daily Press reports a modular-style home installation as a faster rebuild path, with company officials citing factory construction, onsite assembly and lower per-square-foot costs than many custom rebuilds. Worth reading if you are comparing rebuild methods.
  • Prefab construction open houses are scheduled for July 18. This is the actionable follow-up for homeowners curious about systems-built homes: walk active sites, see framing and speak with the company team.
  • Unlicensed contractor allegations remain a practical rebuild warning. Coverage says six men were accused of bidding on Palisades fire-zone construction without proper licenses, with a July 8 arraignment for three and an Aug. 5 preliminary-hearing setting for three others. The resident takeaway is unchanged: verify licenses and be careful with bids and deposits.
